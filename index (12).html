05 Frequently Asked

Questions
clients always ask.

Everything you need to know before we work together. Still have a question? Just message me.

Short reels (under 60 seconds) are typically delivered within 24 hours. Longer edits — YouTube videos, brand films, or multi-segment content — usually take 48 to 72 hours depending on complexity and the number of assets you send.

Send your raw footage, any reference videos or examples you like, the goal of the video (promote, inform, entertain), and any text or captions you want included. A brief voice note explaining your vision also helps a lot.

Revisions are unlimited until you are fully satisfied. I would rather spend the extra time getting it right than have you post something you are not proud of. That said, a clear brief upfront means fewer rounds back and forth.

Instagram and TikTok reels, YouTube long-form videos, podcast video clips, brand and product promos, event highlights, educational content, and documentary-style interviews. If you have footage and a goal, I can shape it into something worth watching.

You can share files via Google Drive, WeTransfer, or any cloud link. WhatsApp works for smaller files too. I will tell you the best option once we chat about your project.

Yes — animated captions, lower thirds, text overlays, simple motion graphics, and colour grading are all part of my workflow. If you need advanced 3D or complex VFX, let me know upfront so we can discuss what is feasible.

For new clients I typically ask for 50% upfront and 50% on delivery. For ongoing monthly work we can agree on a flat retainer. All pricing is quoted clearly before any work begins — no surprises.

Yes. I currently work with creators and brands that need 10 to 30 pieces of content per month. If your volume is higher, reach out and we will figure out a workflow that keeps delivery fast and quality consistent.
